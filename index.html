About Me

Studio practice in the ATLAS digital fabrication lab.

Naz Khani is a creative technologist and designer in the MS Creative Technology & Design program at CU Boulder.

She is a lab assistant in the digital fabrication lab, helping students prototype with tools such as laser cutters, 3D printers, and CNC machines while developing her own interactive installations and design work.
