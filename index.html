Digital fabrication & making

Background, interests, and a glimpse into hands-on work in the lab.

Naz Khani (Fatemeh Hadavandkhani) is a creative technologist and designer in the MS Creative Technology & Design program at CU Boulder. Her work spans interactive installations, UX, and physical prototyping.

She enjoys moving between digital and physical tools: sketching and interface design, coding behaviors, and using fabrication equipment to bring ideas into space.
