About Me

Digital Fabrication & Practice

Studio practice at the CU Boulder ATLAS digital fabrication lab.

Naz Khani is a creative technologist and designer in the MS Creative Technology & Design program at CU Boulder.

She is a lab assistant in the ATLAS digital fabrication lab, helping students prototype with laser cutters, 3D printers, and CNC tools while developing her own interactive installations and objects.
